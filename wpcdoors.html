WPC Bathroom Doors

The most popular product for ready-to-install door applications is WPC Bathroom Doors. Decorative doors can be made using CNC carving and routing on this material. WPC Poly Veneer Doors

FuturWud's WPC Doors with Poly-Veeners. Introducing Poly-Veneered Doors on WPC Doors. Veneers composed of polymeric materials are known as poly-Veneers. PVs don't require any more maintenance or cleaning after polishing or repolishing. PVs work best when mounted on WPC Door surfaces since they are completely water- and termite-proof. For large-scale applications, FuturWud has introduced 10 unique Poly-Veneer (PV) designs.

3 Layer Brown WPC Doors

FuturWud's main product line includes 3 Layer Brown WPC Doors and Brown WPC Door Frames, which are ideal for both public and private sector projects. Due to the fact that they are ready to hang and do not require paint applications, these products are extremely popular with project firms. Products are solid, with door densities of 600 kg/cum and 850 kg/cum for door frames. For fix-and-finish project installations, colours are matched.
