Casement

Casement door is designed in a traditional way that can be opened and closed completely with a sash fixed in outer frame can be closed tightly. There are variable designs in this type and add a glimpse of perfection to your house and you can flaunt your style in front of your guests. They are a one of the perfect modern technologies that give you reliability, durability that works for long time without any complaints. With correct glazing specification, these doors can achieve the energy levels that will make them durable and secured from any weather condition. These doors not only look classy in style but also match easily with any type of home decor.

Sliding Door

Sliding UPVC doors are excellent replacement doors for French and Patio doors. They can transform your entry, exit to a room in your home or simply transform your main entrance to the house. Such doors styles are fast gaining popularity among those who are extending their houses by building a conservatory touching the outside with the inside. Slider doors are best option where you need to open and close quickly. These sashes move horizontally on tracks to open. More sashes can be added to this door where there is a need to open wide using less space. They are priced comparatively low in the market.

Tilt & slide

Futur-Wud Tilt N Slide Doors offer the ultimate in protection for your home. The European concept of tilting and sliding the door sash allows the provision of a dual compression seal and provides the maximum ventilation and glass area. The dual compression seal gives excellent insulation from sound and dust alike.

Slide & Fold

Futur-Wud Fold N Slide Doors system provides superior operating performance and smooth integration into any architectural style and space. The door system is engineered using high quality uPVC Window, Door profiles, superior hinge, and locking systems that are reliable and secure. The rollers, along which the doors slide, offer a smooth glide and reliable performance with solid design and expert engineering.

Lift & Slide

Futur-Wud, the leading fenestration brand in the country, has given innovative fillip to traditional sliding and the result is – Lift n Slide Doors which are designed for larger openings for unobstructed views. By rotating the handle 180° the door fully lifts off its seals. ENCRAFT'S hallmark engineering precision ensures ease of function in both operations, that is, sliding and lifting.

Aluminium-Wardrobe

Aluminium wardrobe designs have been popular among home buyers looking to improve the aesthetics of their bedroom. They are now available in a variety of colours, designs, and sizes to meet everyone’s taste, requirements, and storage demands. Besides improving the rooms' appearance, aluminium wardrobe are highly durable, dependable, and functional. Compared to other furniture types such as wood or bamboo furniture, the aluminium wardrobe stands out due to its portability, light weight, and beauty. In addition to their attractiveness, aluminium wardrobe are available at a reasonable price, which does not pinch the buyers' pockets.
