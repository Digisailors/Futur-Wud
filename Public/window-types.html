Bottom Hung

The main features of bottom-hung windows are that they take up little space when open, provide a constant flow of air and do not intrude in the room where they are fitted. Being both extremely versatile and easy to fit. Giesse bottom-hung windows can resolve numerous ventilation problems whether residential or commercial. The fact that such windows can be opened or closed manually or automatically by remote control also makes FuturWud bottom-hung windows the ideal solution for those bottom-hung windows that are difficult to reach.

Awning / Top Hung

Top Hung windows are like traditional casement windows, with one major difference. Casement windows pivot on hinges mounted on the side, while awning windows pivot on hinges mounted at the top. It can be used independently or in combination with other windows. They are particularly useful for ventilation. They are placed higher in walls which provide natural light and ventilation, without compromising privacy. High placement also allows for maximum wall space for design aspects such as art and furniture placement.

Colours Available :

Fixed windows are the best choice where you need a good outside view and do not have to open.
