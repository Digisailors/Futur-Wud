Casement

uPVC casement windows serve several advantages over the traditional wooden and aluminum windows. Some of these advantages are these windows are maintenance free, easy to install, economical, good looking, available in various designs and finishes, fire resistant, weather resistant, offers excellent water tightness and noise-proof.

Sliding Window

Futur-Wud’s exquisite collection of sliding windows adds panache to your homes. Crafted and designed in contemporary styles to complement your desires and taste, the sliding windows from Futur-Wud can be the most elegant choice for your home décor. With enhanced features including simplicity, architectural enrichment, and easy to use, sliding windows are made by keeping different ideas in mind to cater different needs of different customers.

Bottom Hung

The main features of bottom-hung windows are that they take up little space when open, provide a constant flow of air and do not intrude in the room where they are fitted. Being both extremely versatile and easy to fit. Giesse bottom-hung windows can resolve numerous ventilation problems whether residential or commercial. The fact that such windows can be opened or closed manually or automatically by remote control also makes Futur-Wud bottom-hung windows the ideal solution for those bottom-hung windows that are difficult to reach.

Awning / Top Hung

Top Hung windows are like traditional casement windows, with one major difference. Casement windows pivot on hinges mounted on the side, while awning windows pivot on hinges mounted at the top. It can be used independently or in combination with other windows. They are particularly useful for ventilation. They are placed higher in walls which provide natural light and ventilation, without compromising privacy. High placement also allows for maximum wall space for design aspects such as art and furniture placement.

Tilt and Turn

Adorned with mesmerizing features like versatility and generation-next designing, Tilt & Turn Windows offer more than just supporting environmental causes; they provide a practical way to save energy in buildings. Ideal for modern homes, Window Magic tilt & turn windows are gaining huge popularity now-a-days. Just by turning the handle of the tilt and turn windows once, you can open the top slightly for secure ventilation.

Fixed Window

Fixed windows are the best choice where you need a good outside view and do not have to open. These windows are made of fixed frame and glass is well suited at places where there are beautiful landscapes or fantastic view of buildings. These non-opening designs can prevent unwanted visitors so that can ensure safety of your home and family. Besides, it is very easy to install and its plain design adds a sense of beauty in the room.

Garden Window

Garden windows can come in a variety of configurations, but are most similar to bay and bow windows utilizing a 90-degree box bay configuration, with a picture window in the center and a casement window on either side. Futur-Wud offers a garden window style option with a 90-degree box bay configuration available in our 400 Series and E-Series product lines. For Futur-Wud box bay window, mullion posts lock into a channel in each adjoining casement window for a sturdy, easy-to-install unit. Depending on the series, the wood product can be finished or painted, while the exterior is protected by vinyl, Fibrex® composite or aluminum.
