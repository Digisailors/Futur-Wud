Trending Aluminium Window Design Ideas in India

FuturWud  Windows / Category: Aluminium Windows
Aluminium windows have taken the world by storm thanks to the features and benefits it comes up with. It is now the go-to choice for most offices, residential and commercial buildings out there. If you are planning to get one or multiple aluminium windows installed on your property then you must go through this article, so you can make an informed choice.
Why choose Aluminium Window Designs?
Now that you know what kind of aluminium window designs are out there in the market, it’s important to know what their benefits and features are. So to help you out, here are some of the most common benefits that are associated with aluminium window designs.
  • ■
    Better weather insulation :  Weather is one of the things that can’t be controlled or predicted with 100% accuracy which makes it important to take it into account before choosing a window design for your home. Luckily aluminium windows are great at sealing your home shut from excessive rain, thunderstorm, hailstorm etc. By installing one of the designs mentioned below you can enjoy the rainy view with a hot cup of tea or coffee from the comfort of your home.
  • ■
    Lightweight : Aluminium is a lightweight metal meaning you can move it easily without any problem. It is used to make windows, doors and the frames they are installed in. These windows and doors maximise the incoming light and outside view without adding too much weight to the foundation of the building making it a go-to material choice for most properties.
  • ■
    Durable and Sturdy : Aluminium is majorly used as a packaging material thanks to its ability to form an oxide layer on itself which protects it from rusting while also making it a more durable and sturdy choice when compared to wood or plastic. So if you are looking for a windows design to secure your home as well as make breaking into your home next to impossible then aluminium doors and windows are your best bet.
  • ■
    Better thermal insulation : Nowadays most people are looking for ways to insulate their homes and offices from the harsh summer heat as well as the cold winters, so they are constantly in search of window designs that maximise light while also providing good thermal insulation and that’s where these aluminium window designs come in.
  • ■
    Ecofriendly : Aluminium doesn’t rust easily and doesn’t require excessive maintenance meaning you don’t have to get it replaced frequently. These factors make this lightweight, durable and sturdy metal one of the most ecofriendly door and windows material options available in the market.
Most Popular Aluminium Window Designs
Now that you know what aluminium windows are and what their benefits are, it’s time to learn about the most popular aluminium window designs that are available in the market, so you can make an informed choice for your residential or commercial property.
  • ■
    Sliding windows : When it comes to window designs, architects and designers are looking out for ways to maximise incoming light without being a hindrance in your room and that’s where the sliding aluminium windows shine the most. These types of windows are pretty lightweight and can be as large as a door meaning you can enjoy the complete outside view and that too without leaving the comfort and security of your home. Sliding windows are great at providing thermal insulation and stream in ample natural light making the room look spacious and airy.
  • ■
    Tilt and turn windows : Tilt and turn windows are one of the most popular aluminium window designs  in Europe thanks to their versatile nature but now they have made their way to other parts of the world as well. You can turn and conventionally open them or you can tilt them to control ventilation inside your room. These windows are an ideal solution for bedrooms, kitchens, study rooms and bathrooms, especially in high rise buildings where ease of cleaning, excellent ventilation, thermal and weather insulation are important factors.
  • ■
    Casement windows : Casement window designs are the go-to choice for most homeowners thanks to their ability to blend in with both modern and traditional home designs. Casement windows are highly customizable and can be tailormade according to your taste and likings. You can see these windows widely used in residential as well as commercial spaces thanks to their low cost (compared to other designs) as well as low maintenance, easy to use and lightweight but sturdy design. Apart from that these windows also provide excellent thermal, noise and weather insulation.
  • ■
    Fixed windows : Fixed windows can mostly be seen in offices, malls and other commercial spaces where they need to maximise natural light and outside view without taking up much space in your room. These windows are rarely used in residential properties because of their inability to open and provide proper ventilation. If you are planning to get some installed in your home then make sure to pair them up with other aluminium window designs for proper ventilation. By getting fixed aluminium windows installed, you are maximizing the incoming light as well as the outside view while also insulating your room from weather and noise.
